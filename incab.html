In Cab Checks

  • (1. Enter cab)
    "The wheels are chocked, both parking brakes are applied, My truck & trailer secure"
  • (2. Turn the ignition on)
    "All the warning lights come on"
  • (3. Start the engine)
    "All the warning lights go off, Except for: Parking brake light, Seat belt light & Check engine light, which is a minor defect)"
  • (4. Press Accelerator & Brake pedal, check gauges)
    "All the gauges are working.The speedometer, I will check on the road"
  • (5. Check air pressure gauges)
    "Primary and Secondary tank air pressure is at ____ PSI. Normal working pressure is 80-145 PSI"
  • (6. Activate the indicators)
    "Left turn indicator is working. Right turn indicator is working"
    "Four-way indicator is working"
    "High beam indicator is working"
  • (7. Inspect the steering wheel)
    "My steering wheel has no cuts or damages. Turns left good & right good. There is no extra free-play & stays in set postion"
  • (8. Inspect the driver seat)
    "Driver seat is not cut or damaged, mounted properly & stays in set postion"
  • (9. Inspect the seat belts)
    "The seat belt is mounted Securely on top & Bottom""The belt is not cut or damaged"
    "The Buckle is not cracked or damaged"
    "The tether belt top & bottom mounted securely"
    "The belt buckles in & out properly"
  • (10. Inspect the rear-view mirrors)
    "The driver side top & bottom mirrors are not cracked or damaged, mounted & adjusted properly. My view is clear"
    "The passenger side top & bottom mirrors are not cracked or damaged, mounted & adjusted properly. My view is clear"
    "The both hood mirrors are not cracked or damaged, mounted & adjusted properly. My view is clear"
  • (11. Inspect windshield)
    "The windshield is not cracked or damaged,Properly fitted. My view is clear"
  • (12. Check wipers & washer)
    "My wipers are not missing, cut or damaged.Wipers work on all speeds"
    "Washer Spray works. Wipers clean windshield effectively"
  • (13. Check heater fan speeds)
    "Fan speeds 1,2,3,4 all working"
  • (14. Check defroster)
    "Defroster is working. Clears windshield from the inside"
