In Cab Checks

  • (Enter cab) "The wheels are chocked, both parking brakes are applied, My truck & trailer secure."
  • (Turn the ignition on) "All the warning lights come on."
  • (Start the engine) "All the warning lights go off, Except for: Parking brake light, Seat belt light & Check engine light, which is a minor defect)."
  • (Press Accelerator & Brake pedal, check gauges) "All the gauges are working.The speedometer, I will check on the road"
  • (Check air pressure gauges) "Primary and Secondary tank air pressure is at ____ PSI. Normal working pressure is 80-145 PSI."
  • (Activate indicators) "Left turn indicator is working. Right turn indicator is working."
  • (Activate hazard lights) "Four-way indicator is working."
  • (Activate high beam) "High beam indicator is working."
  • (Inspect steering wheel) "My steering wheel has no cuts or damages. Turns left good & right goof. Thereis no extra free-play."
  • (Inspect driver seat) "My seat has no cut or damaged, mounted properly."
  • (Inspect seat belts) "The seat belt is mounted Securely on top and Securely on Bottom. The belt is not cut or damaged. The Buckle is not cracked or damaged. The belt buckles in & out properly."
  • (Inspect mirrors) "All mirrors are not cracked, damaged, mounted securely, and adjusted properly."
  • (Inspect windshield) "No cracks or damages. Properly fitted. Clear view."
  • (Check wipers) "No cuts or damages. Works on all speeds."
  • (Activate windshield washer) "Spray works. Wipers clean windshield effectively."
  • (Check fan speeds) "Fan speeds 1,2,3,4 all working."
  • (Check defroster) "Defroster is working. Clears windshield from the inside."
