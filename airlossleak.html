Air Loss/Leak- Testing Procedure

  • 1. Enter cab
    "The wheels are chocked, both parking brakes are applied, My truck & trailer secure"

  • 2. Start the engine
    "I will release both parking brakes" ( yellow & Red brakes In)

    • 3. Lower driver windown down, Turn Truck off & Ign ON
    "My window down, truck is off & Ignition is ON,

  • 4. Press & hold Pedal/Service brake
    "My current pressure is ___psi"


  • 5. Start Timer & Listen for possible air leaks for 1 Minute
    "For 1 min, I will listen for possible air leaks"

  • 6. After 10-15sec,
    " I hear no air leak outside, I will continue to listen"

  • 7. After 1 Minute, Stop Timer
    " My current air pressure is ____psi, I lost 2psi in 1 minute Which is better than 4psi"

  • 8. Apply Parking brakes ( yellow & Red brakes Out), Secure Truck & Trailer

  • 9. Read Minor & Major Defects
